Help Guide

How to Use the Temperature Converter

Converting temperatures is easy with our tool. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Enter the temperature value in the input field
  2. Select the current temperature unit (From)
  3. Select the desired temperature unit (To)
  4. Click the "Convert" button or press Enter
  5. View your result below the button

Temperature Scales Explained

Scale Common Uses Reference Points
Celsius (°C) Everyday use in most countries 0°C (water freezing), 100°C (water boiling)
Fahrenheit (°F) Common in the United States 32°F (water freezing), 212°F (water boiling)
Kelvin (K) Scientific measurements 273.15K (water freezing), 373.15K (water boiling)

Common Conversion Formulas

For reference, here are the formulas used in our converter:

  • Celsius to Fahrenheit: (°C × 9/5) + 32
  • Fahrenheit to Celsius: (°F - 32) × 5/9
  • Celsius to Kelvin: °C + 273.15
  • Kelvin to Celsius: K - 273.15

Tips for Accurate Conversion

  • Always double-check your input value
  • Verify the selected units (From/To)
  • Results are rounded to 2 decimal places
  • Negative temperatures are supported
  • Use decimal points for fractional temperatures
